How We Work

Outcome First. Systems Second. Features Third.

We install AI-powered front-desk and follow-up systems that help businesses book more appointments and stop revenue slipping through the cracks — without adding more admin work to your team.

Each system targets one of the four main revenue leaks we see in appointment-based businesses. Most of our clients start with one or two systems and expand from there based on what delivers the most impact for their specific situation.

System 01

Chair Fill System

Reduces no-shows and helps refill cancellations before they cost you money.

Full Details

The problem it solves

No-shows and late cancellations create empty slots, lost revenue, and front-desk pressure. Most businesses have no automated system to prevent or recover from them.

What it does

Smart appointment reminders
Confirmation flows
Reschedule handling
Cancellation recovery messaging
No-response flagging
Booking support

What this helps you achieve

  • → Fewer no-shows
  • → More filled slots from cancellations
  • → Less admin stress on your front desk
  • → Better patient communication without extra effort
System 02

Lead Recovery System

Reactivates old enquiries and turns cold leads into booked appointments.

Full Details

The problem it solves

Most businesses have unconverted leads sitting untouched in their inbox or CRM. These are people who were already interested — they just never got the right follow-up at the right time.

What it does

Lead reactivation campaigns
SMS and email follow-up
Reply qualification logic
Booking support
Hot lead alerts to your team
Follow-up reminders

What this helps you achieve

  • → More appointments from your existing data
  • → Less wasted spend on lead generation
  • → Faster follow-up without manual effort
  • → Clearer visibility on enquiry pipeline
System 03

Missed Call Recovery System

Captures missed calls, responds instantly, and routes hot enquiries to your team.

Full Details

The problem it solves

Busy businesses miss calls. When a call is missed and there is no instant response, the enquiry often goes to the next business that does answer. No voicemail recovery means the revenue is gone.

What it does

AI receptionist (24/7)
Instant text-back
Call summary to your team
Lead capture
Staff alerting
Optional booking link

What this helps you achieve

  • → Fewer lost enquiries from missed calls
  • → Faster response times to new enquiries
  • → Better front-desk coverage without extra staff
  • → More opportunities recovered from existing call volume
System 04

Reputation Engine

Automates review requests, referral prompts, and private unhappy-patient routing.

Full Details

The problem it solves

Most businesses do not ask consistently for reviews or referrals. Unhappy patients can go public before the team has a chance to address the issue. Good experiences go unasked for.

What it does

Automated review requests
Private feedback routing
Referral ask flow
Rebooking prompts

What this helps you achieve

  • → More 5-star reviews generated consistently
  • → More referrals from existing patients
  • → Better reputation control — problems handled privately first
  • → Improved repeat business through rebooking prompts
